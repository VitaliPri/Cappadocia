Hotel Sultan Cave Suites

Perched on top of Aydınlı Hill, this unique hotel offers rock-cut rooms furnished with antique furniture from all over Turkey. The hotel's sun terrace offers beautiful panoramic views of Goreme National Park.

Most of the rooms at Sultan Cave Suites are carved into the rock, providing coolness and tranquility. All rooms have a seating area and a modern bathroom. Most have a private terrace or balcony.

Cave Suites Sultan features a gourmet restaurant serving specialties from the Cappadocia region. After dinner, go to the terrace to enjoy a drink and watch the sun set over the village.
